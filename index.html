How to Play

Overview

One Piece: Card Voyage is a roguelike deck-building game. Navigate the Grand Line, battle enemies, collect cards, and become the Pirate King!

Combat

Each turn you draw 5 cards and get 3 energy. Play cards by clicking them, then click an enemy to target attacks. Block reduces incoming damage. End your turn when done.

Map

Enemy - Fight a regular battle
Elite - Tough fight, better rewards
Rest - Heal or upgrade a card
Shop - Buy cards and potions
Boss - Defeat to advance

Characters

Luffy - Aggressive attacker, combo-based
Zoro - Heavy damage, multi-hit strikes
Nami - Control and debuffs
Sanji - Speed and evasion
